About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Magic Kids Entertainment has been bringing smiles to children's faces for over a decade. What started as a small group of performers has grown into the premier children's entertainment company in the region.

Our team consists of professional actors, dancers, magicians, and educators who are passionate about creating magical moments for children.
